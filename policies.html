Privacy Statement

As an independent developer, I respect the privacy of those who use my software. I do coding as a hobby, and I only make money from in-app purchases. I do not collect any personal information, and I only receive the analytical data that all developers get in the Google Play Console, such as crashes, app not responding, install, uninstall, and revenue reports. Again, these reports do not contain any personal information. In short, I respect your privacy, and I do not collect anything other than what is automatically reported on Android and is sent to the Play Store.

Some More Info:

  • Violoncello - Like all of my other apps, Violoncello does not collect any data that is not automatically sent to all developers who publish their apps on the Play Store, such as ANR and crash reports. I do not collect any personal information in Violoncello or any of my other Android apps.
  • LinkBoard - LinkBoard is no different than my other apps; I collect no personal information, whatsoever.
  • binoc - Same goes to binoc. The only data that I get is what the Play Console automatically reports to all developers. I respect your privacy. binoc uses Glide, an open-source library, to get images from a stock image site called Unsplash. You can find the privacy policy for Unsplash here.
  • Compliment Moi - While Compliment Moi does need to have access to your device's camera to work as intended, this is only used for the main compliment function. I respect your privacy and do not collect any data other than what is sent to all developers who publish their apps on the Google Play Store.
