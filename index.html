Freelance Photography

  Services

I specialize in portrait photography. However, I'm not opposed to photographing casual events. If you have something in mind, you can contact me via the methods listed below.

  Availability

I will be available starting May 2025. That said, you can get in touch to schedule ahead.

At this time, I'm willing to take photos anywhere within a 60 mile radius of Peru, IN.

  Pricing

I charge a flat rate of $20/hour shooting plus $5 for every set of 20 photos edited.

After I take the pictures, I'll send all of them to you to review. Let me know which ones are keepers, and if you want me to, I'll edit them. Again, each set of 20 photos edited costs $5. The $5 rate still applies if you submit less than 20 photos to edit.

I accept cash, checks, and Venmo.

  Why Pick Me?

There are many reasons why you should hire me! Some include:

  • Experience: I've been doing photography as a hobby for years, and I took an advanced photography class at my high school's career center. I learned how to use PhotoShop and Lightroom for various projects.
  • Price: I try to offer affordable photography services.
  • Communication: I value your business and strive to respond punctually and professionally.
